About

I’m Kenneth Ward, and I’m passionate about my work at the GLIDE Foundation, a non-profit in San Francisco. Through social media and visual content, I promote the impactful work GLIDE does. In addition to visual storytelling, I manage the foundation’s website—designing, updating highlights, accomplishments, and services. My career in coding is driven by a desire to enhance our web services and advance my skills. Outside of my professional role, I am a photographer and videographer who has worked independently with non-profits like Code Tenderloin, East Oakland Youth Development Centre, and Dental Robin Hood. I’ve also shot music videos and interviews.
